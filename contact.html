Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

For peak season (December-April), we recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for courses or specialty dives. During low season, 1 week notice is usually sufficient. For same-day bookings, please call or WhatsApp us directly to check availability.

Yes, we offer complimentary pick-up from all hotels on Maafushi Island. For guests staying at resorts in South Malé Atoll, we can arrange boat transfers for an additional fee. Please provide your accommodation details when booking.

For regular dive trips, cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled dive receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before the dive are non-refundable. For courses and packages, please refer to the specific terms provided at booking.

Maafushi is easily accessible from Malé International Airport. Public ferries run daily (except Friday) and take approximately 90 minutes (USD 2). Speedboat transfers are available 24/7 and take 30 minutes (USD 25-30 per person). We can arrange airport transfers for you when you book with us.
