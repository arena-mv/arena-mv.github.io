Discover Scuba Diving

Your first underwater adventure

Experience the thrill of breathing underwater without the commitment of a full certification course. Our Discover Scuba Diving program is perfect for beginners and those curious about the underwater world.

Under the close supervision of our experienced instructors, you'll learn basic skills in shallow water before exploring the vibrant house reef to a maximum depth of 12 meters. No prior experience necessary!

Program Includes

  • Theory briefing
  • Shallow water skills practice
  • 30-45 minute guided reef dive
  • All equipment provided

Program Details

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Available: Daily
  • Max Depth: 12 meters
  • Ratio: 1:2 instructor/student

What to Expect

Your Discover Scuba journey
1

Welcome & Registration

Complete a brief medical questionnaire and registration form. Our staff will help you select properly fitting equipment.

2

Theory Briefing

Learn the basic principles of scuba diving, underwater communication signals, and important safety procedures.

3

Shallow Water Skills

Practice essential skills in shallow water, including breathing through your regulator, clearing your mask, and recovering your regulator.

4

The Dive

Explore the underwater world with your instructor, discovering colorful coral reefs and tropical fish in their natural habitat.

5

Celebration & Next Steps

Celebrate your achievement and discuss options for continuing your diving education with a PADI certification course.
