Safety Protocols

Beyond industry standards

Arena Dive Club maintains a perfect safety record through rigorous protocols that exceed PADI and local requirements.

  • Strict Ratios: Maximum 4:1 student-to-instructor ratio for all courses and guided dives.
  • Mandatory Briefings: Comprehensive site, conditions, and emergency briefings before every dive.
  • Buddy System: Enforced buddy checks and pairing for all divers, regardless of experience level.
  • Surface Support: Dedicated surface coordinator monitoring all dive groups via GPS and radio.
  • Conservative Profiles: Computer-monitored no-decompression limits with additional safety margins.

Equipment Standards

Premium gear only

Maintenance Schedule

All regulators receive visual inspection after every rental and full service every 100 dives or 6 months. BCDs undergo pressure testing quarterly.

Premium Brands

We exclusively stock Scubapro, Aqualung, and Mares equipment with full size ranges to ensure perfect fit for every diver.

Air Quality

Our compressors undergo quarterly testing to exceed EN12021 standards for breathing air quality. All tanks are visually inspected monthly.

Emergency Response

Prepared for any situation

Our team trains monthly in emergency scenarios to ensure swift, effective response to any diving incident.

Medical Support

Direct line to hyperbaric chamber in Malé with 24/7 emergency evacuation protocol.

First Aid

All staff certified in Emergency First Response and oxygen administration.

Every boat carries dual oxygen units, AED, comprehensive first aid kit, and emergency action plan with GPS coordinates for all dive sites.
