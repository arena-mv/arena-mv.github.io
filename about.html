Our Story

Born in Maafushi

Arena Dive Club began as a small jetty-side hut in 2009 with a simple promise: treat every visitor like family and every dive like the most important one of the season.

Today we operate a PADI 5-Star dive center, train divers from Discover Scuba to Divemaster, and guide daily departures across the richest corners of the South Malé Atoll.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Safety First

Strict 1:4 ratios, modern gear servicing, and DAN-trained staff on every boat ensure confident descents for every diver.

Local Stewardship

Weekly reef clean-ups, citizen science ID projects, and reef etiquette briefings keep the Atoll thriving.

Hospitality

From sunrise tea to WhatsApp check-ins, our crew delivers warm Maldivian service from the jetty to the dive deck.
